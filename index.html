About

I am a master's student in mathematics at the Courant Institute (NYU), graduating May 2027. I work in computational algebraic geometry and numerical analysis, and I build mathematical software in Julia. Before NYU I completed a double degree in Mathematics and Systems & Computing Engineering at Universidad de los Andes (Colombia), advised by Mauricio Velasco in mathematics and by Nicolas Cardozo in Systems and Computer Engineering.

Software

HomotopyGetsReal.jl

Code · Documentation

A Julia library for homotopy continuation with a focus on computing and certifying real solutions of polynomial systems. It aims to make real-solution tracking practical for researchers who need both numerical performance and a posteriori certificates.
